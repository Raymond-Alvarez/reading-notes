## Recipe 1: Creamy Florentine Chicken over Rice

This is a classic "skillet" style meal where the sauce is the star.

### Ingredients:

* Chicken
* Spinach
* Heavy Cream
* Rice

### Instructions:

1. **Prepare the Rice:** Rinse the rice and cook it according to package instructions (usually a 2:1 water-to-rice ratio). Set aside.
2. **Sear the Chicken:** Cut the chicken into bite-sized pieces. In a large pan over medium-high heat, sear the chicken until golden brown and cooked through. Remove chicken from the pan and set aside.
3. **Make the Cream Sauce:** Reduce heat to medium. Pour the heavy cream into the same pan, scraping up any browned bits from the chicken. Let it simmer for 2â€“3 minutes until it begins to thicken slightly.
4. **Wilt the Spinach:** Add a large handful of spinach to the cream. Stir until the spinach has wilted into the sauce.
5. **Combine:** Add the chicken back into the sauce. Toss to coat and let everything simmer together for 1 minute.
6. **Serve:** Spoon the creamy chicken and spinach mixture generously over a bed of fluffy rice.

## Recipe 2: One-Pot "Lazy" Creamy Chicken Risotto

This method cooks everything together so the rice absorbs the cream and chicken juices.

### Ingredients:

* Chicken
* Rice
* Heavy Cream
* Spinach

### Instructions:

1. **Brown the Chicken:** Cut chicken into small cubes and brown them in a pot.
2. **Toast the Rice:** Add the dry rice to the pot with the chicken and stir for 1 minute to lightly toast the grains.
3. **Simmer:** Add water (standard amount for your rice) and a splash of the heavy cream. Cover and simmer on low heat until the rice is nearly tender.
4. **The Finish:** Stir in the remaining heavy cream and the spinach. Fold gently until the spinach is wilted and the rice looks thick and "risotto-like."
5. **Rest:** Turn off the heat and let it sit covered for 2 minutes before serving.
